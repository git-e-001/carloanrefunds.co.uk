TO WHOM IT MAY CONCERN

This authority relates to any and all loan accounts or agreements that I had, or have, with your company.

  1. I hereby appoint and authorise Redbridge Finance Limited (CRM43144), to investigate my claim for compensation for the mis-selling of payday loans and other such loans which come under the Financial Conduct Authority’s (FCA) definition of ‘High Cost Short Term Credit’, and if Redbridge Finance Limited believes that it is merited, to act on my behalf to make a complaint and seek compensation for these inappropriately granted loans. I grant this authorisation to allow Redbridge Finance Limited to represent me in accordance with the FCA Handbook’s DISP 2.7.2R regulation which states, “A complaint may be brought on behalf of an eligible complainant (or a deceased person who would have been an eligible complainant) by a person authorised by the eligible complainant or authorised by law. It is immaterial whether the person authorised to act on behalf of an eligible complainant is himself an eligible complainant.”
  2. I authorise and instruct you to co-operate with Redbridge Finance Limited in relation to this claim and to provide to them, whether by telephone, email, or post, any information, whether you deem it confidential or otherwise, which they request during the course of the investigation and claim, without delay.
  3. I authorise and instruct you to accept information from Redbridge Finance Limited, including responding to requests for information, information provided through them by me, and the particulars of any complaint made.
  4. I grant Redbridge Finance Limited the right to use their experience and expertise to decide the best way to manage my complaint, but not the rights to make decisions regarding my financial position, make financial decisions or decide whether to accept an offer of compensation, if awarded.
  5. I authorise that this authority to be extended to cover all loan, “rollover”, “extension” or “top up” agreements that I hold, or have held, with you.
  6. I understand that, in addition to the present Letter of Authority, I will need to provide further information when raising an expression of dis-satisfaction to the Lender, about the underlying product(s), service(s) and where known, specific account number(s) being complained about. Doing so will enable the Lender to assess and determine the complaint as quickly and as effectively as possible.
  7. I authorise and instruct that all payments in settlement to be made to Redbridge Finance Limited and confirm that only payments so made will be accepted in full and final settlement of the claim.
  8. Having lawfully entered into a contract with Redbridge Finance Limited I expressly consent, and require, that all communications be made directly to them.
  9. I confirm that a photocopy, or an electronic copy, of this Letter or Authority will have the same legal effect as the original.

Sign only if you accept the terms of this Letter of Authority. This agreement is signed electronically, signing electronically constitutes legal acceptance and gives authority and instruction to lenders and other relevant parties for Redbridge Finance Limited to act for you in the same way as if you had signed it with a pen.
